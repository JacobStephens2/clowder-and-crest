# What to Share with Model Council

## Recommendation

Share the **GitHub repo** directly: https://github.com/JacobStephens2/clowder-and-crest

This gives them access to all source code, assets, and documentation. They can also play the live game at https://clowder.stephens.page.

## If they need a single-file summary, share these 3 documents:

1. **`source_documents/Clowder-and-Crest-Design-Document.md`** — comprehensive design doc covering architecture, all features, game flow, economy, progression, and what's implemented vs deferred. This is the most complete single document.

2. **`source_documents/Story.md`** — the full narrative: prologue, 5 chapters, bond conversations, themes, Catholic mythlore. Shows the creative vision.

3. **`README.md`** — quick feature list, tech stack, and how to run it.

## Key questions to ask Model Council:

- What would make a player want to come back tomorrow?
- Is the economy balanced? (15 fish start, 2/cat/day upkeep, jobs pay 5-30 fish)
- Are there enough meaningful decisions per day, or does it feel like going through the motions?
- Which of the 4 minigames is weakest and how could it improve?
- What's the biggest gap between what the story promises and what the gameplay delivers?
- How should the reputation system (Crest/Shadow) be expanded to feel meaningful?
- What would Chapter 6+ content look like?
