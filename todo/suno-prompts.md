# Suno Sound Prompts — Cat Sounds for Clowder & Crest

## General Style
Medieval atmosphere, pixel art game, cozy-but-somber tone. SFX should feel warm, organic, slightly lo-fi.

## Cat Vocalizations (per breed)

### Wildcat
- **Meow:** Short, rough, assertive meow. Feral confidence. Think stray cat with attitude.
- **Purr:** Low rumbling purr, steady, grounding. The sound of earned trust.
- **Hiss:** Sharp defensive hiss, territorial. Used when something threatens the guild.

### Russian Blue
- **Meow:** Soft, quiet, almost whispered meow. Reserved and gentle.
- **Purr:** Gentle, high-pitched purr. Soothing and constant.
- **Chirp:** Small curious chirp, like noticing something interesting.

### Tuxedo
- **Meow:** Precise, clipped meow. Businesslike, formal.
- **Purr:** Measured purr with a rhythmic quality. Controlled contentment.
- **Trill:** Quick satisfied trill after completing a task.

### Maine Coon
- **Meow:** Deep, resonant meow. Surprisingly gentle for the size.
- **Purr:** Deep chest rumble, loud, like a small engine. Warm and reassuring.
- **Chirp:** Big low chirp, friendly greeting sound.

### Siamese
- **Meow:** Loud, dramatic, drawn-out meow. Vocal and insistent.
- **Purr:** Chattery purr mixed with small vocalizations.
- **Yowl:** Dramatic yowl for attention or emphasis. The most vocal cat.

## Game SFX

### UI Sounds
- **Fish earned:** Light coin-like clink, organic (shell or bone on stone)
- **Job accepted:** Quill scratch on parchment, quick stroke
- **Day advance:** Distant church bell, single toll, reverberant
- **Chapter complete:** Small medieval fanfare, lute + bell
- **Puzzle block slide:** Wooden thud/scrape on stone floor
- **Puzzle complete:** Satisfied cat purr + small chime
- **Toast notification:** Soft wooden tap

### Ambient
- **Rain on stone:** Gentle rain, cobblestone surface, occasional drip
- **Hearth fire:** Crackling fire in a stone fireplace, warm
- **Cathedral bells:** Distant church bells, atmospheric
- **Market bustle:** Muffled human voices, cart wheels on cobblestone
