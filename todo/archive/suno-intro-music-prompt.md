# Suno Prompt — Intro Scene Music

## Track: "The Stray's Arrival"

**Style:** Ambient medieval, solo lute or harp with rain sounds, melancholic but hopeful

**Prompt:**
Slow ambient medieval music, solo lute plucking a simple melody over gentle rain on cobblestones. Melancholic but with a hint of hope underneath. Quiet, reflective, like a lone traveler finding shelter on a stormy night. No drums, no vocals. Fades in from silence. 60-90 seconds. Think Kingdom Two Crowns opening atmosphere meets a rainy night in a medieval town.

**Mood progression:**
- 0-20s: Just rain ambience, very quiet lute enters
- 20-50s: Simple melody establishes, warm but sad
- 50-70s: Melody lifts slightly — a sense of determination
- 70-90s: Resolves gently, fades out

**Tags:** ambient, medieval, lute, rain, atmospheric, melancholic, hopeful, pixel game
