# Fight Minigame Music Prompt

For generating a combat music track for the Brawl minigame in Clowder & Crest.

## Prompt

Medieval tavern brawl music, fast-paced Celtic combat theme. Urgent bodhrán drum rhythm with aggressive fiddle melody. Minor key, 140 BPM. Tension building through waves with brief breathing moments between surges. Pixel art retro game aesthetic — chiptune-influenced but organic instruments. 30-45 seconds, loopable. Think medieval Zelda combat meets Irish pub fight. No vocals.

## Style References
- Zelda: A Link to the Past — combat encounter music
- Stardew Valley — mine levels (urgent but cozy)
- Kingdom Two Crowns — attack themes (atmospheric medieval)
- Celtic battle music with bodhrán and tin whistle

## Technical Requirements
- Format: MP3
- Duration: 30-45 seconds
- Must loop seamlessly
- Volume-matched to existing puzzle tracks in assets/audio/
